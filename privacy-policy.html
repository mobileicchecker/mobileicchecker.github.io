MIC App Privacy Policy

MIC App values and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We use several Firebase and Google services that may automatically collect certain types of technical and usage-related information.

1. Firebase Analytics

Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • Device information
  • App usage events
  • Session data
  • Country/region information

This information is used only to improve app performance and user experience.

2. Firebase Cloud Messaging (Push Notifications)

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) may collect:

  • Device token
  • Notification delivery information

This data is used only for sending app notifications.

3. Firebase In-App Messaging

  • App events
  • Device information
  • User interaction with messages

Used to display in-app informational messages.

4. Firebase Remote Config

  • App version
  • Device information
  • Configuration and feature settings

Firebase Remote Config is used to dynamically manage app behavior, feature availability, update requirements, ad controls, and related settings.

5. Firebase Firestore Database

Our application uses Firebase Firestore to store and retrieve non-personal IC-related data.

  • IC codes and related technical information
  • Trending IC searches
  • Popular search suggestions
  • Unknown or unavailable IC searches

The app may anonymously store searched IC codes and search frequency information to:

  • Improve search suggestions
  • Show trending ICs
  • Improve future database updates
  • Enhance search accuracy

We do not collect or store:

  • User names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Passwords
  • Personal identities
  • Location data

Search tracking is completely anonymous and used only for app improvement purposes.

6. Firebase Crashlytics

Firebase Crashlytics may collect:

  • Crash logs
  • Device information
  • App version
  • Stack traces

Used only for fixing crashes and improving app stability.

7. Firebase App Check

Firebase App Check is used to help protect the app and backend services from abuse, unauthorized access, and automated traffic.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements inside the app.

AdMob may collect and process certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data
  • App usage information

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your region and consent choices.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we request user consent before serving personalized advertisements in accordance with GDPR.

Users may:

  • Accept or reject personalized ads
  • Change consent preferences where applicable

Google and its advertising partners may process data according to their own privacy policies.

More information:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
